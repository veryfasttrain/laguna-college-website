ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Basic Education Students (K-12)

NEW STUDENTS AND TRANSFEREES:
  1. Report Card
  2. Good Moral Certificate
  3. ESC Certificate (for incoming Grade 11 students from private schools)
  4. Completion Certificate (for incoming Grade 11 students from public schools)
  5. Copy of PSA Birth Certificate
  6. Two 1x1-sized photos with navy blue background

OLD STUDENTS:
  1. Report Card
  2. One 1x1-sized photos with navy blue background

College and Graduate Studies

NEW STUDENTS AND TRANSFEREES:
  1. Report Card
  2. Good Moral Certificate
  3. Copy of PSA Birth Certificate
  4. Two 1x1-sized photos with navy blue background

GRADUATE STUDIES:
  1. Transcript of Records
  2. Certificate of Employment
  3. Marriage Certificate (for married female students only)

OLD STUDENTS:
  1. One 1x1-sized photos with navy blue background

ENTRANCE EXAMINATION PROCEDURE:

  1. Take the Laguna College Entrance Test Application Form.
  2. Wait for an e-mail from the school with payment details and instructions on how to send proof of payment.
  3. Once payment is confirmed, the Guidance Office Personnel will e-mail you the LCET Guidelines and Exam Schedule.
  4. Take the test on the scheduled date and time. Your examination results will be sent via e-mail in 3 working days.
